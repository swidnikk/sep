Business Advising

Work with 1 on 1 business advisors who will give you actionable, relevant growth strategies & connections to the resources you need to accomplish them.

OUR APPROACH

We provide affordable membership based access to highly experienced 1-1 advising, networking, education, referrals & resources for value based Sonoma County businesses. We work with local, established businesses who need to go to the next level of growth or startups who need to assess the viability of their idea to learn their next steps. We promise solid business guidance and mentorship to support business resilience & community here in Sonoma County.

If you want help growing your business, or wonder if your new business has potential, apply for a free consultation and see if you qualify for affordable, solid business guidance and mentorship 

Here's what you can expect after completing the inquiry form below

1

ASSESSMENT

Your inquiry is reviewed and assessed, a discovery call is scheduled.

2

SIGN UP

A lead advisor is assigned and you will meet to review your objectives and challenges.

3

ACTION PLAN

An action plan created with clear next steps tailored to your goals and objectives.

4

REGULAR CHECKINS

Regular checkins with advisors for timely advice, introductions, and resources.
