Helping local businesses thrive — enjoy life, change our world, make money

That’s our philosophy here at SEP, and yes, in that order. Because we believe that a good business is one that supports enjoyment of the things that matter, while making a contribution to the world we share. And that we all earn a living wage while we do it. We are here to support local entrepreneurs to grow their business and stay viable in order to create greater resilience & community here in Sonoma County.
